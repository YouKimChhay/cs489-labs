Software Requirements:

- The system should allow the office manager to register new dentists.
- The system should allow the office manager to enroll new patients.
- The system should allow the office manager to review and book appointment requests.

- The system should allow patients to submit an appointment.
- The system should allow patients to view their appointment.
- The system should allow patients to change or cancel their appointment.

- The system should allow dentists to view a listing of all their appointments.

- The system should maintain data about surgery locations.
- The system should send confirmation emails to patients.
- The system should verify the appointment requests:
	- the dentist has less than 5 appointments in the given week.
	- the patient has no outstanding or unpaid bill.
